# CreatorTime Whitepaper
## Tokenizing Creative Time

**Abstract**

CreatorTime introduces a novel blockchain protocol that tokenizes creative time.

## 1. Introduction

The creative economy represents over $2 trillion in global value.

## 2. How It Works

1. **Register** as a creator
2. **Verify** your creative work
3. **Earn** CTK tokens
4. **Build** reputation

## 3. Tokenomics

- **Total Supply**: 10,000,000 CTK
- **Daily Reward**: 1 CTK per creator
- **Reputation System**: 100-300% multiplier
